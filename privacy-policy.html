Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us.

This Privacy Policy explains how our app collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Information We Collect

When you use our app, we may automatically collect certain non-personally identifiable information about your device and usage. This can include your device type, operating system version, app version, country or region, and how you interact with the app (e.g., which screens are viewed and for how long).

Analytics

We use TelemetryDeck to collect anonymized analytics data. This helps us understand general usage patterns and improve the performance and user experience of our app. TelemetryDeck does not collect personally identifiable information and is designed with user privacy in mind.

How We Use the Information

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Monitor app performance
  • Understand user engagement and usage trends
  • Improve app features and functionality

Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties. Analytics data collected through TelemetryDeck is processed in a privacy-focused manner and not shared with advertisers or other external parties.

We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal request.

Data Retention

We retain anonymized analytics data only as long as necessary to support the ongoing development and improvement of the app.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal obligations. Updates will be posted within the app or on our website.
